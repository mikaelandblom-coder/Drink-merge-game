🎵 Sound Lab

Auditioning merge sounds for the music-shop map. Each tier maps to a scale degree — climbing tiers climbs the scale. Winner gets lifted into audio.js.

A · Music box / celesta

Sine fundamental + bright inharmonic bell partials. Toy-like, twinkly, clearly "musical."

B · Plucked string (ukulele / harp)

Sawtooth through a decaying lowpass with a tiny noise pluck. Warm, organic, folk-instrument feel.

C · Marimba / vibraphone

Triangle fundamental + strong 4th harmonic (marimba's signature). Round, woody, mellow.

🪙 Coin & collision (Melody Lane)

The coin shower plays an ascending pentatonic run (one octave above the merges) instead of a repeated blip — a small merge is a short rise, a big combo payout is a long harp cascade. Collisions get a soft hollow-body knock instead of a metallic tink. Coins land ~110ms apart, matching the real game tempo.

🏆 Personal best (high-score flourish)

Plays on the game-over screen when a run beats that variant's best (the game's fanfare(), currently map-agnostic). "Current" mirrors what ships today; the others are alternatives — the harp and brass options would also tie into Melody Lane's musical theme.

Tip: hit "Play cascade" on each card to compare how a 9-chain combo sounds — that's the sound you'll hear most in a good run. Then try a couple of single tiers to judge the timbre up close.
